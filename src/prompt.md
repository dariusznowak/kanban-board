# Overview

Hundreds of thousands of travel agents around the world use Sabre Red to
shop, book, and service travel reservations for their customers. Using
the Sabre Red Software Development Kit (SDK), you can build Red Apps
that integrate directly into an agent’s Sabre Red. Customizing to fit
your unique needs has never been easier.

Use the Sabre Red Software Development Kit to develop Red Apps for your
agency and for the Sabre Central Marketplace. The Sabre Red Software
Development Kit was created to facilitate Red App development. Here’s
what you’ll find in the SDK:

-   Runtime

-   Tools

-   Documentation

-   Sample code

Here’s what’s in it for you:

-   Access to hundreds of thousands of agents around the world through
    the Sabre Central Marketplace

-   Evolve your business and find new sources of revenue

-   Provide your best marketing pitch and set your own price point in
    the Sabre Central Marketplace Admin Centre

## [](#_red_app_developer_tools)Red App Developer Tools

## [](#_documentation)Documentation

Want to know more? Check out the SDK documentation on our

## [](#_sabre_central_marketplace)Sabre Central Marketplace

Sabre Central Marketplace is a single self-service marketplace to shop
and purchase innovative travel solutions built on the Sabre platform.
Red Apps developers can access SCM Admin Centre to submit proposals,
upload new versions, manage Red App’s storefront, and much more.

-   [Sabre Central Marketplace](https://central.sabre.com/marketplace)

-   [Sabre Central Marketplace Admin
    Centre](https://marketplace.sabre.com/admin-centre)
